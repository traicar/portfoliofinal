Healthcare Worker Turned Techy!

How I started coding: I have been working as a physical and occupational therapist since 2008, with a lot of studying in between! My husband is an IT professional and it always amazes me the things that he creates just by sitting at his computer. While I was at home, without a job, during the pandemic, he just casually told me to try coding. I also was always curious of why the medical records I documented on everyday could not be made more efficient. So I decided to take up the challenge. I never realized when I went from coding for fun to falling in love with it! I believe in life long learning, and I saw coding as a good intellectual challenge! I enrolled in a bootcamp and did a lot of self studying. Here I am today, a full stack developer, all ready to dive in into the tech world!

Life outside of work: I am always ready to pack my bags and explore this beautiful world, every chance I get. I have visited 34 countries so far and hope to keep adding many more to the list! I love cooking and experimenting with different ingredients during my spare time. One of my priced possessions is a camera and enjoy taking pictures, mostly during my travels and of the food I make. A good book and a cup of tea is the definition of a perfect evening for me! I am a big proponent of green living and incorporate green habits in my day to day life to reduce my carbon footprint. Above all, I absolutely enjoy learning new skills and find myself learning something new all the time.
